About Croplink

Built on Agriculture. Driven by Opportunity.

Croplink is an agricultural trading company connecting quality crops from Malawi with reliable local and international markets.

We work across the agricultural supply chain, from sourcing and quality control to storage, logistics and distribution. Our goal is to build dependable partnerships while creating greater opportunities for farming communities.
